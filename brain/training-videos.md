# Appointment Setter Training Videos

8 topics locked 2026-06-22. Brayden finds the YouTube videos for each, transcribes them, and sends the transcript text to Eagle/Falcon who then generates:
- Flashcards tied to that video's content
- Quiz questions covering the material
- CC then wires them into the Training Center (unblocks the long-standing Task 2)

## The 8 Topics (in order)

1. **Cold calling mindset** — rejection, consistency, the numbers game, staying sharp after a rough stretch
2. **Tonality & delivery** — how you sound matters more than the words; warmth, pacing, confidence
3. **The discovery script** — why questions beat pitching, how each branch works, what you're listening for
4. **Getting past the gatekeeper** — reaching the owner without tipping your hand or pitching the wrong person
5. **Handling objections** — "not interested," "we're good," "send me an email" — word-for-word responses
6. **Qualifying the prospect** — what makes a lead worth booking vs not, reading pain signals, missed calls vs avg ticket
7. **Booking & handoff** — how to frame Nate as the specialist, the two-time close, what a confirmed appointment sounds like
8. **Time management & call discipline** — structuring the 150-lead day, staying in flow, knowing when to move on
