# Review Agent — Standalone Lead Strategy

## The Insight
Review Generation (Product 3) has no Indeed titles currently capturing it — and Indeed isn't even the best channel for it. A Profile A business with under 50 Google reviews OR under 4.2 stars is a warm lead regardless of whether they're hiring. They have the problem; they just don't know AI solves it.

## Lead Sources (Phase 2 — activate after sales machine runs)
1. **Maps scraper (existing)** — already scrapes Profile A businesses. Add a review-count + rating filter: flag any business with <50 reviews or <4.2 stars as a Review Agent prospect. No new tool needed.
2. **Indeed expansion** — titles 14-16 (marketing assistant, social media coordinator, reputation manager) now capture this in the scraper.
3. **Manual trigger** — any Maps lead with no website AND low reviews = Elite tier conversation (website + review agent bundle).

## Pitch Angle
Never mention competitors. ROI anchor: "A business jumping from 3.8 to 4.5 stars on Google typically sees 15-20% more inbound calls from the map pack — without spending a dollar on ads. The AI does the asking automatically after every job."

## Stack Mapping
- Standalone: fits into Pro tier and above (Product 3 included at $797/mo+)
- Upsell angle: any Maps lead already getting a website (Vertical 2) is a natural Review Agent add-on
- Elite natural fit: website + receptionist + review agent = full presence package

## When to Activate
Phase 2. Right now the Maps scraper already flags no-website businesses for Vertical 2. The review-count filter is a one-line addition to that same scraper — park it until Vertical 1 has 5+ recurring clients.
